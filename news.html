R.I.P Peter Tork

21 February 2019

Peter Tork, coming from one of the most iconic bands during the 1960's; The Monkees, had sadly become the second Monkee member who has passed away at the age of 77.


The death was confirmed by his son, Ivan Iannoli, according to The New York Times. Tork's son had mentioned how the cause was complications of a rare form of cancer that was first diagnosed in 2009.
